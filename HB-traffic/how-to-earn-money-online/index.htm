Ways To Earn Money From Blogging.

The three simple way to earn money from blogging includes:

1. Google Adsense:

Google AdSense is one of the best and most popular ways to earn money from Blogging. With Google Adsense, you can have ads placed on your blog pages and articles and get paid handsomely by Google. 

There are millions of dollars to be made from Google Adsense annually, depending on the volume of traffic that visits your blog daily. Also, there are thousands of people making cool money from ads displayed on their blogs You should start earning a living online through blogging too.

2. Affiliate marketing:

Many businesses, corporations and Multinational companies like Amazon and Flipkart run Affiliate programs to sell their product or service. 

Affiliate marketing is a system of consciously signing an affiliation agreement with a company or brand to advertise and refer to their products and services, by including their offers on your blog for a specified commission on sales or a clearly defined action.

By joining their Affiliate Program, you can place banners of their product or service on your blog. When visitors click on those banners on your blog and buy the products, you get a commission from those companies. It’s as simple as that. With this bloggers are earning a lot of money nowadays.

To get started, create and register an affiliate account with any company or retailer of your choice, and obtain an affiliate link that would be inserted at strategic areas on your blog pages, posts and articles. With this, you can start to earn money online.

3. Sponsorship:

Another simple way to make money online through blogging is to accept special sponsorship contracts from brands, businesses and anyone in need of a good public placement. 

Many bloggers do this and make a lot of money. When your blog becomes popular, many companies would ask you to review their product by direct contact, in return for which they are ready to pay you good money.

How To Earn Money Online Through YouTube

With Google, life has never been better. Thanks to Google, you can make money online. If you are creative and have a sense of engaging people with beautiful content and videos, then YouYube is your best option.

On YouTube, almost anything is content. You can create a YouTube account, build a subscriber base, share captivating videos, and earn money from view on your videos. If you have any skills like singing or dancing and you are good at it, you should have a YouTube Channel.

Creating an account does not require any money or payments. There are many niches to pick from if you decide to make money online and earn a living on YouTube. 

To qualify for monetization, you must meet the standard requirements from YouTube to start earning money online. The most recent and prominent of these standards is to have a minimum of 1,000 subscribers and to pass the 4,000 watch hours threshold. 

Once qualified, you can apply for monetization. When approved you will start getting paid for clicks and actions on ads inserted on your videos by YouTube.

That’s not all. You can also include links and mentions of your affiliate marketing in your videos, or video descriptions on YouTube. That they can buy the product by clicking on the link given in the description, and you get paid in commission. 

How To Earn Money Online Through Affiliate Marketing

Outside of Blogging, Affiliate marketing can earn you lots of money too. Beyond what I have mentioned above, let me give you more details about Affiliate Marketing. This is what you should know.

Affiliate marketing is a program that runs by very big companies like Amazon, Flipkart etc to promote their product or service. Now, let’s understand affiliate marketing, and how can you make money through it.

Basic Tips For Affiliate Marketing

  1. First, you have to choose a Niche. What product or service you can give a good review on?
  2. You have to run a Blog, A YouTube channel or a very engaging social media space in that Niche to write an article or create content to promote the product.
  3. Then, you should join the company running Affiliate Products associated with your Traffic and from there you can place the product’s banner code on your blog.
  4. When people come to read those articles or content on your website, channel or social media spaces, make sure there is a clear call to action to initiate a purchase.
  5. Ensure your review is not biased and too much above the line in praise of the product. You might lose the interest and trust of your audience who may think your review has been bought with a price.

With every purchase after clicking on your affiliate link, you get a guaranteed commission from the company. That’s how to earn a living online through affiliate marketing.

How To Earn Money Online By Selling Product

Thanks to the internet, you can sell almost anything online and make your money without lifting a finger. Well, not literately. If you have a business or you have a physical store, this will do you much good.

And if you don’t, just research to find out the best product in demand and start to make money online. This may require a little bit of money as capital for the business. 

How to Start Selling Products Online

  1. Carefully decide on the niche and products in high demand within your comfortable capital base.
  2. Create social media accounts on Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, LinkedIn or Twitter, etc.
  3. Constantly create and share videos, pictures and posts on your social media pages to engage and draw the attention of people online.
  4. Have a good logistic plan for proper delivery of goods and services from your store to your customers ahead of purchases.
  5. Prepare a good system of payment and verify payments from your customer for goods and services, in anticipation of sales.
  6. Take clear and crisp videos or images of your products at intervals to initiate sales.
  7. Keep the social media pages busy with activities and regularly advertise your products and services to initiate sales.

There are many online stores on social media without any physical stores. You too can earn a living online selling products.
